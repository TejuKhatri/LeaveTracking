How do I submit a leave request?
Navigate to your dashboard and click "Submit Leave" or use the navigation menu. Fill out the form with your leave details and submit for approval.
Can I edit my leave request after submission?
Yes, you can edit leave requests that are still in "Pending" status. Once approved or rejected, requests cannot be modified.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive instructions to reset your password.
Who can I contact for urgent leave matters?
For urgent leave-related issues, contact your HR department directly. For system technical issues, use our support email or phone number.
How do I update my profile information?
Go to your Profile page from the navigation menu and click "Edit Profile" to update your personal information.
