Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when using the LeaveTrack Management System:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, department
  • Account Information: Username, password (encrypted), role designation
  • Leave Request Data: Leave dates, reasons, types, and status information
  • Usage Information: Login times, system interactions, and activity logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the leave management service
  • Process and track your leave requests
  • Communicate with you about your account and requests
  • Generate reports for administrative purposes
  • Improve our system security and functionality

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared within your organization as follows:

  • Administrators can view leave requests and employee information for management purposes
  • HR personnel may access data for payroll and compliance requirements
  • Supervisors may view leave information for team planning

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encrypted password storage using industry-standard hashing
  • Secure database access with role-based permissions
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access logging and audit trails

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Leave request data may be retained for compliance and record-keeping purposes as required by law or company policy.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information through your profile
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account (subject to legal requirements)
  • Receive a copy of your personal data

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and ensure proper system functionality. These cookies are necessary for the operation of the system and cannot be disabled.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
